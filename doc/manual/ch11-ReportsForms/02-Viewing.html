Viewing Reports

Reports can be viewed at the Tools->Reports menu. they are organized by functonal groupings. Reports are listd on the left and reports on the right. To view a report:

  1. Press Tools->Reports.
  2. Select the tab where the report is located.
  3. Select the Report or Form to view.
  4. Press the Open icon to open the criteria selection window.
  5. After entering the criteria desired, press the Print or Export CSV icon (reports only) to view the report or form.
  6. After viewing, printing or dwonloading the report/form. Press the cancel icon on the cirteria window to close the window.
